## üîπ Description
AI Health Buddy is a **multimodal PartyRock app** designed to help users analyze their medical reports quickly and effectively.  
It provides:

- **Personalized analysis** of lab results with age- and gender-specific ranges.  
- **Health Score** calculation and urgency assessment.  
- **Diet and lifestyle recommendations** tailored to lab results.  
- **Specialist recommendations** with direct Google Maps links.  
- **Dynamic AI-generated images** reflecting health status.  
- **Humorous interactive chat** to motivate users and provide guidance in a casual, encouraging tone.  

This app is **educational** and **not a replacement for medical consultation**.

## üìù How to Use
1. Open the [Live App](https://partyrock.aws/u/ayeshamemon/PNTLs0e5V/DrAi.sha4U)  
2. Enter your **Age**, **Gender**, and **Location**.  
3. Upload your **medical report as a PDF**.  
4. Click **Run** to generate:  
   - Lab analysis tables (Normal / Borderline / Critical)  
   - Health Score & Urgency Level  
   - Key precautions  
   - 7-Day diet plan  
   - Daily habits to improve health  
   - Google Maps links to specialists and pharmacies  
   - Adaptive health status image  
5. Interact with the **AI Health Coach chatbox** for advice and motivational tips.
